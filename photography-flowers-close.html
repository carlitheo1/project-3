Photography Series: Flower Close-Up

Photo two from my photography project at LSU. The series I included focused on the architecture and nature that surrounds campus while also experimenting with the framing of these objects. In this image, the subject is two Louisiana azaleas that is surrounded by a sea of greenery. The photo is a close-up shot of the flowers and highlights the light and darkness of the location that was photographed.
