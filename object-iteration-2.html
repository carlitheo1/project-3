Object Iteration Spread #2

Part two of my object iteration book, which centers around the topic of music. The book focuses on both the physical and non-physical aspects of music and the feeling it creates for the listener. In this spread, I focused on the feeling of music while focusing on the shapes associated with it. The circular form represents that of a stereo speakers as the sound is pounding through the plastic and flowing through the air. The colors and saturation were enhanced in photoshop to create a more immersive experience that this associated with listening to music.
