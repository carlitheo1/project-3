Graphic Response Spread #1

Part one of my graphic response book spread. This book is a time capsule of current event from the last ten years with heavy emphasis on 2020-2022. The book has a consistent style due to its black and white coloring and focuses on a range of topics that were found in the news. In this spread, I depict the initial hysteria the media created during the COVID-19 pandemic and the discussion of the end of daylight savings time. I used a mix of text and imagery to illustrate the main theme of each page and provide links to the original news story.
