Musology App Mockup

Mockup for the fictional app Musology. The app uses data collected from user brainwave to curate playlists and colors based on their mood. The mockup includes different screens that the user would see when using the app including the opening and welcome screens, a graph that shows the person's mood on different days of the past week, a list of curated playlist, and the music screen. The design also used various gradients to represent the noise of one's mind
