Seasonal Palette: Tokyo

Poster for a color palette based on the seasons in Tokyo, Japan. The poster selects a specific flower that blooms during certain seasons in Japan and highlights five of the main colors that flower encapsulates. My plants include winter peonies, cherry blossoms, sunflowers, and autumn foilage / leaves. The poster also takes into account the color of the environment that houses these flowers as well (i.e. sunflowers are typically surrounded by blue skies so the circle surrounding the flower is sky blue). The poster also combines both hand drawing with digital coloring in Adobe Illustrator.
