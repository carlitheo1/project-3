The Six Principles of Design

Mockup of a mini brochure that illustrates the six basic principles of design. The pocket sized brochure is meant to be carried around and serves as a reminder of how to create successful designs. I only used one color (purple) to add boldness to the original black and white design. The six principles depicted are space, scale, form, texture, direction, and weight, with the back cover of the booklet being a logo that represented me without specifically stating my name. This booklet is a digital rendition of my original hand drawing which I brought to life in Adobe Illustrator.
