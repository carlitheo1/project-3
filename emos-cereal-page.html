EMO's Cereal Branding

Mockup for a fictional cereal brand that is focused on the emo subculture. The mascot is a red dinosaur with blue eyes who sports a black, mid-2000s fringe haircut and lives in an apocolyptic, volcanic wasteland. In front of him is an image of the circle (which is also black to match the theme of both the branding and the inspired subculture). The box follows the guidelines of the generic Kellog's cereal box but with a satirical twist!
