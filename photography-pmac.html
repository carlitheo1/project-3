Photography Series: PMAC

Photo one from my photography project at LSU. The series I included focused on the architecture and nature that surrounds campus while also experimenting with the framing of these objects. In this image, the subject is the side of the PMAC and the interesting shapes displayed on the side of the building and on the roof. The photo creates a framing contrast between the sky and the man-made structure.
