Object Iteration Spread #1

Part one of my Coffee Poster Series. Dubbed "Catch Up", this poster was created in the first half of 2022 when the Covid pandemic was still looming over most of our heads. This poster encourages people to go to a coffee shop with a loved one to strengthen social skills and get a discount. The poster is a mix between hand-drawing and digital coloring and displays the hands of a mother and daughter holding one another and making up for time lost during the pandemic.
