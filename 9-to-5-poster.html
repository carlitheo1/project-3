9 to 5 Movie Poster

My version of the poster for the 1980's movie "9 to 5". The style of the poster is urban 90s and is reminiscent of Paula Scher's work, while the focus of the poster is centered on the main storyline of the movie (the presumed death of the main antagonist of the movie). My colors were based on the boxes used in the movie (the coffee and rat poison boxes) as well as something that is easily mixable to get other colors.
